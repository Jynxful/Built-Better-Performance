What We Do

Services

01

Routine Maintenance +

Oil changes, brake jobs, cooling systems, fluid flushes — keep your BMW dialed in.

  • Full synthetic oil change & filter
  • Brake pad & rotor replacement
  • Cooling system overhaul (water pump, thermostat, hoses)
  • Transmission & differential fluid service
  • Spark plugs & ignition coils
  • Serpentine belt & tensioner
  • Multi-point inspection included with every service
02

Engine Swaps +

Full engine pulls and swaps. N54, S54, B58 — whatever platform, we've done it.

  • Complete engine removal & installation
  • Wiring harness adaptation & integration
  • Custom motor mounts & fabrication
  • ECU tuning & coding for swapped motors
  • Drivetrain & transmission matching
  • Cooling & oil system upgrades for new platform
  • Popular swaps: N54→E46, S54→E46, B58→E-chassis
03

Turbo Installs & Forced Induction +

Single turbo builds, upgraded twins, supporting mods, and tuning support.

  • Single turbo kit installation & routing
  • Upgraded twin turbo setups (TD04, 20G, etc.)
  • Supporting mods: fuel pump, injectors, FMIC
  • Custom downpipes & exhaust fabrication
  • Oil feed & return line routing
  • Boost leak testing & validation
  • Tuning support (MHD, Bootmod3, custom)
04

Performance Bolt-Ons +

Downpipes, intakes, intercoolers, exhaust — bolt-ons to full-send power packages.

  • Catless / high-flow catted downpipes
  • Cold air intakes & charge pipes
  • Front-mount intercooler upgrades
  • Full turbo-back exhaust systems
  • Blow-off valves & wastegate upgrades
  • VRSF, Wagner, ER, Vargas — we install all brands
  • Stage 1, Stage 2, Stage 2+ packages
05

Suspension & Handling +

Coilovers, bushings, sway bars, alignment — corner balance to track-ready setups.

  • Coilover installation & adjustment
  • Lowering springs & strut combos
  • Control arm & bushing refresh
  • Sway bars & end links
  • Camber plates & adjustable arms
  • Corner balancing & alignment
  • Brands: KW, BC Racing, Bilstein, Ground Control
06

Diagnostics & Coding +

ISTA, MHD, Bootmod3 — proper diagnosis and coding, not guesswork.

  • Full ISTA diagnostic scan & report
  • Fault code reading & root cause analysis
  • Module coding & feature activation
  • DME flashing & tune installation
  • Injector index reset & adaptation
  • VANOS relearn & idle adaptation
  • Pre-purchase inspection diagnostics
07

Towing & Transport +

Flatbed towing for your BMW — whether it's a breakdown, a pickup, or getting your build to our shop.

  • Flatbed towing (lowered & modified cars welcome)
  • Local & long-distance transport
  • Breakdown recovery & roadside pickup
  • Event & track day car transport
  • Project car pickup from sellers
  • Safe loading for wide-body & slammed builds
  • Serving the Inland Empire & surrounding areas
